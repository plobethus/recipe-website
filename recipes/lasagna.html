World's Best Lasagna!

This lasagna is considered the world's best by allrecipes, due to its customizability

  • Meat: Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Cheese: Parmesan, Mozarella, and riccota
  • One onion and two cloves of garlic cooked in the meat to add flavor
  • Tomatoes: One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Spices and Seasoning: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Sugar: two tablespoons of sugar
