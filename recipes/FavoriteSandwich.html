How to make My Favorite Sandwich!

Ingredients!!!!

  • Dave's killer Bread (big slice not thin sliced
  • Cajun seasoned turkey breast (can be found at kroger, boars head is very nice
  • Mild Cheddar Cheese (thic cut)

      • Instructions!!!!

      1. Take two slices of bread out of the bag. Place one on the plate.
      2. Place two slices of cajun seasoned turkey on the bread.
      3. Place two slices of cheddar cheese ontop of the turkey
